This project main goal was to analyse data about football in order to predict the result of each match.

Top 7 european 1st division championships (England, Spain, Italy, France, Germany, Portugal, Netherlands) were under study on a time period of 19 seasons (from 2000 to 2019). The data comes from different sources but mainly from a free nonetheless quite reliable one : football-data.co.uk, from where the database was built and which could be updated every week.

Several forecasting models where developped based on multiple features analysis, including the study of dynamic rating systems and the bivariate Poisson distribution, a simple and solid basis for predictive models. Luckily the data includes odds from serveral gambling websites, allowing to work on betting simulation as well.
